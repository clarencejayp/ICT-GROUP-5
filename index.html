The Youth That We Are Today


The youth that we are today
All fearless, full of hope
Some may be held back by the past,
And get terrified of the future, Yet remains trying, firm, and finding their meanings.

We, a product of all the time's misery, are ready to flee;
Eager to seep out the should-be-forgotten memories,
Keen to face the fate that lies ahead of us.
With our minds all thinking alike,
Changes that the world needs will arise.

Dreams will be the power that we'll use,
Our voices and talents are the weapons,
The shields are the knowledge and experience we get along the way.
And now we're ready to get bruised,
Because that is how it is to be
The youth that we are today.

Group 5
